A goal-focused iterative design process led me to create the All Inclusive Cliff House; a geographically isolated home that incorporates all aspects of independent, off-the-grid living including adequate social, work, and leisure space.
FINAL
Focus on distinguishing elements of the site/house including the expansive view, minimal energy use, and careful distribution of space supported the inclusion of new ideas that addressed the issues [resource scarcity and social isolation] associated with off-grid living.
PRELIMINARY
Developing an off-grid house offered a variety of challenges in terms of the access to resources and amenities. The design of the All-Inclusive Cliff House’s was inspired by Emilio Ambasz's green wall, Louis Kahn’s Kimbell Art Museum, and Richard Serra's sculptures which all emphasized on scale.
