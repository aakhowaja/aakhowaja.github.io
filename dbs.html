The final product was developed to be a socially and culturally vibrant bus station that provided efficient, affordable intercity transportation for refugees moving back to the recently liberated city of Fallujah, Iraq.
FINAL
Of the three design concepts, the hexagon/octagon-inspired design was most viable due to its structural efficiency and proportion to the site.
PRELIMINARY
Since the Fallujah Bus Station was planned to be a regional icon of post-war reconstruction, it was imperative to develop a structure with locally sourced designs and materials that was located at the heart of the city near the central market.
